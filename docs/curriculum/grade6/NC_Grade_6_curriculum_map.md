## Sprint 0 Retro-Tag Note (Grade 6 specific)

Grade 6 is the **entry point** for Rising Stars division. The distribution should lean heavily toward:
- `cognitive_demand: procedural` (majority)
- `complexity: low` (more than Grades 7–8)
- `context: abstract` for fluency generators; `context: real_world` for ratio/percent/statistics

Championship heats for Rising Stars should max out at DOK 2–3 reasoning, not exceed Grade 6 standards.
