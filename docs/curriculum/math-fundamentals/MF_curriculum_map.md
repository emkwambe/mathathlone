# Math Fundamentals — MathAthlone Curriculum Map
**Course:** Math Fundamentals  
**MathAthlone Division:** Foundation (pre-Rising Stars entry tier)  
**Role:** PIVOT COURSE — prerequisite for Grade 6, Grade 7, Grade 8, and Math 1  
**Standards Alignment:** CCSS Grades 3–8 + N-RN, N-Q, A-CED (spans multiple grade levels by design)

---

## Why This Course Is Architecturally Different

Math Fundamentals is **not a single-grade course**. It is a **vertical prerequisite layer** that:

1. Serves Mathletes entering at any division (Rising Stars through Varsity)
2. Provides the bedrock skills assumed-without-reteaching in every downstream course
3. Contains some concepts (Radical Operations, Sets) that unlock **Math 2 and AP Pre-Calc**, not just middle grades
4. Is the **only place** Radical Operations are formally taught before Math 1

Every other course in the platform can be thought of as **built on top of a subset of Math Fundamentals**. Gaps here propagate forward with compounding effect.

*A Mathlete who cannot pass these has no pathway forward regardless of which course they enter.*

| Unit | Concepts | Downstream Impact |
