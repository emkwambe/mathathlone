## Division Context

NC Math 3 sits **above the existing Math 1 (Varsity)** pool and **below AP Pre-Calculus/AP Calculus**. It is the most conceptually rich course in the NC pathway — spanning six distinct domains that each feed directly into college-level mathematics.

**Critical architectural note:** The Trigonometry unit is the **longest prerequisite chain** in the entire platform. A Mathlete who reaches AP Calculus without solid unit circle and trig equation skills will fail the derivatives-of-trig section immediately. Every trig generator here should be treated as AP pipeline infrastructure.
